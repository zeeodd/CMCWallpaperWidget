Instructions

(1) 

Select an image from the sidebar or Upload your own.

(2) 

Add a stamp shape (square, circle, polygon) via the buttons below the Input Canvas.

(3) 

Translate and scale your stamp as much as you want.

(4) 

Click the Crop button once you are satisfied with the size and location of your stamp - this will lock in your choice, so click the Restart button if you wish to start over.

(5) 

On the Output Canvas, you can also translate, scale, and additionally rotate your stamp. Click the Duplicate button to instantly copy/paste one of your selected stamps.

(6) 

If you have one stamp selected, you can right-click and choose to reflect it vertically or horizontally.
